This year (feb) I will complete my task of running the six marathon majors.

In 2004 I ran my first marathon in New York and ended up on crutches. Since then I've run eight proper marathons ranging from a hideous Boston experience, two marathons in a week to 'celebrate' my 30th birthday and achieved a Guinness World Record. I've completed a wine tasting marathon, an ice skating marathon and have dressed up in drag far too may times to be normal. Along the way I've had the privilege of being accompanied and supported by friends and family. Tokyo in Feb 2016 fulfills my target of running the six marathon majors.

I appreciate many of you have been extraordinarily generous over the years so you should feel no obligation to donate. For those of you who would like to contribute to a good cause then you can donate via paypal here. Your money will go towards Recovery Support for the 2011 Great East Japan Earthquake.

I have prepaid the minimum charity amount of £700. I will be sponsoring myself £200 as well as covering all other costs. You can donate via paypal to help me cover the extra £500 as a minimum and hopefully raise even more. I will update this page as and when I can with the total amount donated.
